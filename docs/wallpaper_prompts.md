# Wallpaper Generation Prompts

Prompts for generating Windows 98-era desktop wallpapers. Target aesthetic: the kind of images that shipped on wallpaper packs, OEM desktops, and shareware CDs in 1997-2000. Mix of stock photography, raytraced 3D, and abstract art. 1024x768 or 16:9 widescreen.

---
